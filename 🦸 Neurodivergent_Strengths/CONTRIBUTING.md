## ğŸ¯ Guidelines

### For Dyslexic Contributors
- Use clear, simple language
- Break up long text with headings
- Add visual examples
- No worries about perfect spelling!

### For ADHD Contributors
- Small, focused PRs are perfect
- Incomplete ideas are welcome
- Link to issues for context
- No pressure on timelines

### For Everyone
- Be kind and supportive
- Celebrate neurodiversity
- Share your "why" - what problem does this solve?
- Test your changes if possible
